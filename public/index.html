About

Table Hopper is a website founded by a Student in Temasek Polytechnic while he was on his break. He was disappointed that Yelp and other websites did not helped much when he was finding a restaurant in Singapore, as the design was either too compact or held too little info. Thus, he built a website that allows other to use and also find out more about the restaurant that they want to visit.
